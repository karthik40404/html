Gucci

Gucci, founded in 1921 by Guccio Gucci in Florence, Italy, has become one of the world's most renowned luxury fashion brands. Known for its high-quality craftsmanship, innovative designs, and distinctive logo, Gucci has maintained its status as a symbol of opulence and sophistication. The brand offers a wide range of products including clothing, handbags, shoes, accessories, and fragrances. Over the years, Gucci has evolved, blending traditional Italian artistry with contemporary fashion trends. Under the creative direction of Alessandro Michele since 2015, Gucci has embraced a bold, eclectic aesthetic, capturing the imagination of a new generation of fashion enthusiasts.
